Sierra Wireless Innovation Summit is first of all an exchange between the technical developer community and the IoT business and marketing community. During the whole day, live demos and real use cases presentations and announcements are made by partners and customers of Sierra Wireless.

This year, open hardware themes influence widely the agenda. Be prepared for big announcements by Sierra Wireless and partners!

A large room in the agenda is made for networking and demos allowing business and technical people to talk, exchange, learn, and build the future of IoT.
