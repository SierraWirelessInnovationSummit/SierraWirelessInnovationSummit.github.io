Overview of the last event (June-17 2015)



Sierra Wireless® Innovation Summit 2015, held in Paris last June 17th, is above all a platform for exchange between the technical developer community and the IoT business and marketing community. This year, open hardware themes have take up a substantial part of the agenda, with the announcement of the next-gen WP wireless processors and project mangOH, an open source reference design for IoT applications.

Throughout the day, partners and customers of Sierra Wireless have given live demos, and presented real use cases.

In the afternoon, 2 technical tracks offered hands-on keyboard sessions which allowed developers to build a simple IoT end-to-end application using Project mangOH, open source software and the M2M cloud service AirVantage. The business track has featured real commercial use cases and passionate discussions on future IoT technologies.

The agenda also made ample room for networking and demos, allowing business and technical attendees to exchange, learn, and build the future of IoT.

Thank you all for this succesfull event!
