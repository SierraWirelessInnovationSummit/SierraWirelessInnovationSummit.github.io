Event Overview



Sierra Wireless Innovation Summit is above all a platform for exchange between the technical developer community and the IoT business and marketing community. This year, open hardware themes have take up a substantial part of the agenda, with technical sessions and business cases with open source hardware project mangOH™, and open source framework Legato™.

Throughout the day, the IoT ecosystem give live demos, and presents real use cases.

In the afternoon, several technical tracks will offer hands-on keyboard sessions and allow developers to build simple IoT end-to-end applications using Project mangOH and Legato open source projects, as well as the IoT acceleration platform AirVantage. The business track will feature real commercial use cases and round table discussions on future IoT technologies.

The agenda will also make ample room for networking and demos, allowing business and technical attendees to exchange, learn, and build the future of IoT.
