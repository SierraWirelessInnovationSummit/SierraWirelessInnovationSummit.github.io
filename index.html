LET'S BUILD I°T



Let’s build i(o)t is the theme for the fourth annual Sierra Wireless Innovation Summit. If you have an interest or a stake in the future of the Internet of Things, don't miss this opportunity to meet and network with our large developer and business communities!

At the Sierra Wireless Innovation Summit, you can
  • ▶ learn from some of the most innovative players working on solutions for the Internet of Things,
  • ▶ discover new technologies for IoT solution development
  • ▶ find out how our customers and partners have deployed intelligent IoT solutions
  • ▶ test your skills in hands-on IoT application development
  • ▶ be inspired by 30 live technology demos
  • ▶ network with one of the world's largest IoT developer and business communities.

We hope you will come.
