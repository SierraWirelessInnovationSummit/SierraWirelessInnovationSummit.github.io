LET'S BUILD I°T



Let’s build i(o)t is the theme for the fourth annual Sierra Wireless Innovation Summit. If you have an interest or a stake in the future of the Internet of Things, don't miss this opportunity to meet and network with our large developer and business communities!

At the Sierra Wireless Innovation Summit, you can
  • ▶ learn from some of the most innovative players working on solutions for the Internet of Things,
  • ▶ discover new technologies for IoT solution development
  • ▶ find out how our customers and partners have deployed intelligent IoT solutions
  • ▶ test your skills in hands-on IoT application development
  • ▶ be inspired by 30 live technology demos
  • ▶ network with one of the world's largest IoT developer and business communities.

We hope you will come.

Anne Bouverot is Chair and CEO Anne brings to Safran a global experience in IT, telecoms and digital. Anne is a graduate in telecommunications engineering of the Ecole Normale Supérieure, and holds both a telecommunications engineering degree and a PhD in computer science (1991).

   Keynote - Security in IoT
Plenary session - 11:00-11:20
IoT clearly has the potential to enable us to lead smarter, healthier and generally more efficient lives. Everything is about seamless connectivity and management of huge flows of data. This huge variety of services will call for multiple categories of devices that will use very different network capacities compared to the current mobile phones and tablets. Anne Bouverot, CEO of Safran Identity & Security, will present how the first challenge of connectivity can be addressed and how we can leverage on it to enable IoT to safely deliver on its promise of greater efficiency for manufacturers, operators and consumers protecting data.
